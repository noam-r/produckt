ProDuckT is a free, open-source AI-powered platform that helps organizations create professional, strategy-aligned Market Requirement Documents (MRDs) quickly and collaboratively.

Get Started on GitHub Learn More

Powerful Features for Product Teams

Everything you need to create comprehensive, professional MRDs

Distributed Intelligence

Distribute MRD creation to the right experts across Business Development, Technical, Finance, Operations, and Product teams. AI Editor unifies insights into a polished document.

RICE & FDV Scoring

Built-in evaluation and scoring modules assess each initiative's readiness, risk, and priority. Know when you're ready to build with confidence.

Hours, Not Weeks

Generate complete, professional MRDs in hours instead of weeks. AI-powered question generation and document synthesis accelerate your product process.

Strategic Alignment

Ensure products align with company goals, KPIs, and success criteria. Set organizational context once and maintain consistency across all initiatives.

Cross-Functional Collaboration

Role-based access control ensures the right people answer the right questions. Break down silos and align input into one coherent narrative.

Professional Quality

AI Editor transforms scattered insights into polished, professional MRDs that meet enterprise standards. Ready for stakeholder review and decision-making.
